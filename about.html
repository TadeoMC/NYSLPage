Mission

To suport young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to larn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills arround team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Vision

The Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful atheltes through the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

General Information

The Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes resideing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an enviroment in wich to leartn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
